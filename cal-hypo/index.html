FRA technical approval required

Using calcium hypochlorite in a fertilizer stock concentrate is case-specific. Confirm the product, concentration, injection order, and operating conditions with FRA technical before use.

DryTec handling and mixing
  • Use clean, dry tools.
  • Add DryTec to water. Never add water to DryTec.
  • Wear chemical-resistant gloves and eye protection.
  • Avoid breathing dust and use adequate ventilation.
  • Store DryTec cool and dry in its original container.
  • Keep DryTec away from acids, organic material, and other incompatible products.

Calculated chlorine values are theoretical applied doses. Field decisions use measured free chlorine at the applicable endpoint.
